01 · Organize

Bring order without rearranging your work.

Group loose, unpinned tabs by matching site. Existing groups stay intact, and single-site tabs remain loose instead of creating one-tab clutter.

  • One click or Ctrl+Shift+G
  • Optional grouping for newly opened tabs
  • Idle sweeps report grouped and skipped tabs
02 · Refine

Turn site names into useful topics.

When Chrome's built-in Prompt API is available, Group Tabs can classify eligible tabs into categories such as Work, Development, or Research. Processing stays on-device; there is no cloud fallback.

  • Topic names are customizable
  • Manual groups and pinned tabs stay untouched
  • Site grouping remains available without AI
03 · Recover

Reopen the missing parts of a session.

Save a grouped window under a name. Restore later into a new window; URLs already open elsewhere are skipped and the result reports exactly what came back.

  • Small manual sessions may use Chrome Sync
  • Large sessions stay on this device
  • Automatic recovery keeps three local snapshots

Private by architecture

No analytics. No account. No developer-operated backend.

Group Tabs makes no remote API calls. Chrome Sync may carry small manual sessions through your browser account; automatic recovery stays local. Read the full storage contract.
